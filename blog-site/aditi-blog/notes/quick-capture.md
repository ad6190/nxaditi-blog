# Quick capture

I try to capture ideas as soon as they show up: a line in the phone notes app, a voice memo, or a quick line in Obsidian. The goal is to get it down before it evaporates. Refinement happens later.

## Where it goes

- Inbox in Obsidian
- Then either into a note, a writing draft, or the archive
