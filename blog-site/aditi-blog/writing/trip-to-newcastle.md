i went to newcastle for work and had a weekend to spare. what followed was equal parts food trail, city walk, and quiet reflection.

## good food, good city rhythm

i spent that weekend walking and eating my way through the city. japanese one meal, lebanese the next, then vietnamese after that. newcastle has that rare quality of feeling calm and alive at the same time.
